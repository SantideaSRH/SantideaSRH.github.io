About

Functionality

Enemy Behavior:

  • Enemies and the boss move horizontally across the screen, changing direction when they hit the screen edges.
  • They periodically fire bullets downwards towards the player.
  • Each enemy has a health bar above them that depletes as they take damage.

    • Collision Detection:
  • Player bullets destroy enemies by reducing their health.
  • Enemy and boss bullets reduce the player's health.
  • Sound effects play for both hits.

    • Health Management:
  • Both the player and enemies have health bars. When an entity's health reaches zero, it is defeated.
    • Win Condition:
  • You win the game by defeating all three enemy types (the two regular enemies and the boss).
    • Loss Condition:
  • lose if your player ship's health reaches zero.
    • Visuals & Sound:
  • A static background sets the scene, while scaled images represent the player, enemies, and bullets. Audio cues enhance the immersive experience.

    • Key Features

    Classic Arcade Shooter Gameplay: Engaging 2D top-down shooting action.
    Intuitive Controls: Easy-to-learn WASD movement and spacebar shooting.
    Multiple Enemy Types: Face off against two distinct enemy ships and a powerful boss, each with unique attributes.
    Health System: Player and enemies have visible health bars for strategic combat.
    Projectile Combat: Dynamic bullet mechanics for both player and enemies.
    Sound Effects: Audio cues for shooting and hitting targets enhance immersion.
    Clear Win/Loss Conditions: Understand your objectives and consequences readily.
    Powered: Built on the versatile Pygame library for robust game development.
