How To Play

Your primary goal is to destroy all three enemy units—two regular ships and a powerful boss—each with its own health bar. Be careful, as enemy bullets will reduce your ship's health; if your health reaches zero, it's game over, but if you defeat all foes, victory is yours!

Controls & Actions

Controls

  • W: Move upwards
  • S: Move downwards
  • A: Move to the left
  • D: Move to the right
